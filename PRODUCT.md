# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Three visitor types, all arriving cold from a link (CV footer, DM, pitch email), each giving the page 30–90 seconds:

- **Seed investors** — scanning for signal: is this founder real, is the product live, is there a moat?
- **Design partners** (logistics SMEs) — practical buyers: does this person understand freight, can I trust the software?
- Secondary: recruiters and potential co-founders assessing raw capability.

The job to be done: within 5 seconds, conclude *"this is a serious, unusually capable builder"* — then find the receipt trail that proves it, and a clear way to get in touch.

## Product Purpose

A bilingual (EN/VN) founder story site for a 2nd-year logistics student who solo-built ARISE — a live, three-service AI logistics-intelligence platform (OMEN → VANTIS → NEXQUOTE) — in ~7 months by directing AI coding agents under a self-designed engineering discipline. The site is itself Exhibit A: its craft must demonstrate the same discipline it describes. Success = investor/partner contact, CV downloads, zero "student project" dismissals.

## Brand Personality

Calibrated, load-bearing, quietly luminous. First person, calm confidence; specific over boastful — every claim ships with its receipt. Technical-but-human: the human narrative voice (display type) and the machine evidence voice (mono readouts) are kept deliberately distinct on the page.

## Anti-references

- Generic resume/portfolio templates (photo left, skill bars, star ratings).
- SaaS-cream landing pages and purple-gradient "AI startup" heroes.
- Terminal cosplay: fake CLIs, scanlines, ASCII borders, mono-everything.
- Editorial-magazine affectation: italic display serif, drop caps, ruled three-column broadsheet grids.
- The hero-metric template (big number + tiny label grids) and identical icon-card grids.

## Design Principles

1. **Proof over adjectives** — never say "impressive"; show the live signal, the scorecard, the CI badge. Claims are captions for evidence.
2. **The diagram is the handshake** — the animated OMEN→VANTIS→NEXQUOTE chart introduces the founder before a single paragraph is read.
3. **Two voices, one page** — human narrative in large grotesque type; machine evidence in small mono readouts. Never blended.
4. **Nothing decorative that isn't informative** — motion and color always encode meaning (data flow, liveness, risk), never garnish.
5. **Both languages are first-class** — VN is a full parallel voice, not a translation afterthought; proper nouns and tech terms stay English in both.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA: contrast ≥4.5:1 body text in both themes, full keyboard navigation with visible focus, semantic landmarks/headings, alt text on all imagery, `prefers-reduced-motion` honored with crossfade/static fallbacks (the hero diagram must remain legible with motion off), `<html lang>` correct per locale, 360px→4K responsive with no horizontal overflow.
